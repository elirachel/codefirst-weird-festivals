Ludswigsburg Pumpkin Festival

Do you love pumpkins? Well Halloween has come early! Ludwigsburg in Deutschland is graciously hosting the biggest pumpkin festival (arguably the only) festival in the world. The gracious vegetable is shown to be incredibly versatile through the festival’s yearly theme. This year is Rome – get your chariots ready. Carving is not the only vocation available in Ludwigsburg Pumpkin festival, for example:
  1. Pumpkin large-scale art
  2. Gourmet styled pumpkins (pumpkin popcorn anyone?)
  3. Entertainment! Live music, artistic carving
  4. Stuff relevant for children if you are so inclinded
  5. There's a shop also obviously
