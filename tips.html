Tips for Festivals

  1. Don’t forgot your PASSPORT!!!!!!!! You need that
  2. Or money – how else you gonna buy fancy gifts for all your friends
  3. Bring some form of phrasebook for the country, or learn please and thank you before you go (don’t want to ruin the politeness stereotype)
  4. If staying in a hostel: Pillow case – don’t bother bringing a pillow just stuff your dirty clothes
  5. Don’t wear a onsie – don’t be that guy
  6. Bring a bag you can carry your day stuff in that isn’t your main travel bag, as you don’t want to have to carry round lots of extra stuff
  7. Make sure to stock up and prepare for the commodity that you’re going there to enjoy, be it tomatoes or pumpkins, you don’t want to be the only person without a tomato to smash.
  8. Wetwipes, always useful, always applicable. Especially if you’re taking part in the tomato festival.
  9. Portable phone charger – just in case it runs out while you’re on the move. The city you’re visiting may even have citymapper
  10. Bring an arm wallet – can’t trust anyone these days
  11. Remember to book your hotel/hostel far in advance, as it will get very busy
